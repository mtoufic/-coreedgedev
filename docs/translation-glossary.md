# Core Edge bilingual translation glossary

This glossary keeps Arabic localization consistent across the Core Edge website.

## 1. Terms that must stay English

Do not translate these terms, names, handles, URLs, numbers, units, or address lines:

- Core Edge
- Core Edge Developments
- AURA
- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- Facebook
- Email addresses, phone numbers, URLs, and social handles
- West Square Mall
- SODIC West Beverly Hills
- Giza
- Egypt
- 20
- 279
- 10.5K
- m²
- G+2
- 10+
- 85%
- 24.7%
- Signature when used in `Signature Villas`
- Flagship Development
- Low-Density
- Exclusive
- Gated

## 2. Terms that may be Arabic

Use natural, premium Arabic rather than literal translation:

- Private Community → مجتمع خاص
- Residential Compound → مجمع سكني خاص
- Developer → المطور
- Project → المشروع
- Why Green Belt → لماذا الحزام الأخضر
- Contact Us → تواصل معنا
- Green Belt → الحزام الأخضر
- West Cairo → غرب القاهرة

## 3. CTA translations

- Reserve Your Villa → احجز فيلتك
- Discover AURA → اكتشف AURA
- Contact Us → تواصل معنا
- Book a Private Viewing → احجز معاينة خاصة
- Download Brochure → تحميل البروشور
- Speak With Us → تحدث معنا
- Message Us On WhatsApp → راسلنا على WhatsApp

## 4. Footer/contact rules

Keep the footer contact, social, and address section mostly in English and uppercase where used in the design:

- WHATSAPP
- INSTAGRAM
- FACEBOOK
- SALES@COREEDGEDEV.COM
- West Square Mall · 2nd Floor · S31
- SODIC West Beverly Hills · Giza · Egypt
- Legal footer only: Core Edge Development & real Estate 2026 ©

Do not translate platform names, email addresses, URLs, or social handles.

## 5. Location naming rules

- Keep official address lines in English.
- Keep `Giza` and `Egypt` in English in address blocks.
- In marketing copy, `الشيخ زايد`, `6 أكتوبر`, and `الحزام الأخضر` are acceptable for natural Arabic flow.
- West Cairo must be rendered as `غرب القاهرة` in Arabic copy.

## 6. Number/unit rules

Keep numbers and units exactly as written in source content:

- Use `20`, not Arabic words.
- Use `279 m²`, not translated or expanded units.
- Keep `G+2`, `10+`, `85%`, `24.7%`, and `10.5K` unchanged.
- Do not localize phone numbers or WhatsApp links.
